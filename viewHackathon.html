Renewable Hack'19

Last updated 3 mins ago

Renewable Hack 2019 is a 24 hour Hackathon based on Renewable Energy sources like Solar, Wind, Tidal, Hydral etc. This Hackathon is in association with JHUB and co-partners are HYSEA, NEN, CII so that the selected teams of this hackathon are promoted to JHUB for idea development. So, Grab this wonderful oppurtunity to turn your idea into implementation and win exciting cash prizes!! Registrations for Hackathon are here. Registrations end on 4th January'19.

The Important dates to be remembered for Hackathon are:
03rd January - An Idea Formation talk by Ms.Radhika Meenakshi to drive up the idea for Hackathon.
05th-06th January - A WorkShop/BootCamp to tell about relevant technologies to be used in hackathon, all necessary guidelines. Read the prerequisites in the BootCamp poster and make sure to be ready with them.
10th-11th January - The 24 hour Renewable Hack.
Be present for all the above to gain the knowledge and to know more about renewable energy.
