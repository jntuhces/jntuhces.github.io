Renewable Hack'19

Last updated 3 mins ago

Renewable Hack 2019 is a 24 hour Hackathon based on Renewable Energy sources like Solar, Wind, Tidal, Hydral etc. This Hackathon is in association with JHUB and co-partners are HYSEA, NEN, CII so that the selected teams of this hackathon are promoted to JHUB for idea development. So, Grab this wonderful oppurtunity to turn your idea into implementation and win exciting cash prizes!! Registrations for Hackathon are here.

The Important dates to be remembered for Hackathon are:
03rd January - An Idea Formation talk by Ms.Radhika Meenakshi to drive up the idea for Hackathon.
04th January - A WorkShop/BootCamp to tell about relevant technologies to be used in hackathon, all necessary guidelines.
7th-8th January - The 24 hour Renewable Hack.
Be present for all the above to gain the knowledge and to know more about renewable energy.
